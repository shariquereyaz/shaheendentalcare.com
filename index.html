Services

Center for wide variety of Dental and Maxiloo-Facial Treatments!

Orthodontic Treatment

The use of devices to move teeth or adjust underlying bone.

Braces

Metal ,Ceramic and Lingual.

Root Canal Treatment

Save a tooth that is badly decayed or becomes infected. During a root canal procedure, the nerve and pulp are removed and the inside of the tooth is cleaned and sealed.

Micropigmentation for Leucoderma

A technique in which minute, metabolically inert pigment granules are implanted below the epidermis for cosmetic and/or corrective enhancement.

Tooth Removal

It is a relatively quick outpatient procedure with either local, general, intravenous anesthesia, or a combination.

Bleaching

Whitening teeth beyond their natural color. Active ingredients like hydrogen peroxide and carbamide peroxide are bleaching agents most often used in teeth bleaching processes.

Tooth Whitening

Tooth whitening lightens teeth and helps to remove stains and discoloration.

Invisalign

Invisalign aligners are the clear alternative to metal braces for adults and teens.

Tooth Jewellery

A cosmetic dental procedure where a diamond or a stone is attached to the teeth.

Denture(Fixed/Removable)

A removable plate or frame holding one or more artificial teeth.

Teeth Placement

Surgical procedure that interfaces the bone of the jaw or skull to support a dental prosthesis such as a crown, bridge, denture, facial prosthesis or to act as an orthodontic anchor.

Smile Designing

Cosmetic treatment to improve the appearance of your smile through procedures such as dental veneers, composite bonding, teeth whitening and tooth implant.

Digital X-Ray

Sensitive plates to directly capture the tooth structure.

Facial Scar Removal

Common scar removal treatments include: Dermabrasion. Uses a high-speed brush or other instrument to resurface your skin and remove or reduce the depth of scars.
